 $ f_{nl} $ : Coefficient considering non-linear effects, to be taken as:

• For extreme sea loads design load scenario:

 $$ f_{n l}=0.7at f_{x L}=0 $$ 

 $$ f_{n l}=0.9at f_{x L}=0.3 $$ 

 $$ f_{nl}=0.9at f_{xL}=0.7 $$

• For ballast water exchange design load scenario:

 $$ f_{nl}=0.85at f_{xL}=0 $$

Intermediate values are obtained by linear interpolation.

 $ f_{yz} $ : Girth distribution coefficient, to be taken as:

 $$ f_{yz}=\frac{Z}{T_{LC}}+f_{yB}+1 $$

 $$ f_{h}=\begin{array}{r}3,0(1,21-0,66\ f_{T})\end{array} $$

 $$ k_{a}=(0,5+f_{T})\left\{(3-2\sqrt{f_{y B}})-\frac{20}{9}f_{x L}(7-6\sqrt{f_{y B}})\right\}+\frac{2}{3}(1-f_{T}) $$
